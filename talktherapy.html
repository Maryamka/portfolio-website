Talk Therapy

Overview

A mental health app that provides you a safe place to share your thoughts, feelings, and experiences.

My Prosess

Problem statement

After extensive industry research, it became clear that most mental health apps focus on features e,.g. the ability to talk to a licensed mental health professional, relaxation techniques, tracking user’s behaviors, and many more. There are no voice-chatting apps where people can speak about their problems and support each other. Since the Corona crisis and the passage of new laws such as social distancing, self-help organizations can’t pursue their activities, and society needs more wellbeing support.

Proposal

The data showed that mental health disorders are common everywhere. Improving awareness, recognition, support and treatment for this range of disorders should therefore be an essential focus for global health. I want to create a voice-chatting mental health app that focuses on building a supportive community for people dealing with mental health issues.

User Flow

After understating users’ needs and goals, I focused on the main tasks users need to accomplish while interacting with the app and defined 3 user flows.
