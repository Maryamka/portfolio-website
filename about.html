About Me

My Story

My name is Maryam, and I am a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating aesthetically pleasing designs to meet users' needs and business goals. I have experience in financial service and sales, which has provided me with valuable skills such as understanding user's needs and goals, empathy, communication, problem-solving, and collaboration. After following my passion for photography and visual design, I realized my interest in the ways people interact with technology and products, especially in the e-commerce industry. Through my research, I became familiar with the field of user interface design, and I was keen on learning more. I began learning user interface design by enrolling in Careerfoundry Bootcamp. Now, I am looking forward to designing for brands to make a positive impact on people's lives and also make digital products easier and more pleasing to use.
